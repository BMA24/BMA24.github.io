The Family Behind BMA

A Family Dedicated to Building Bridges

BMA Consultancy was born from a vision to create more than just business connections—to build family bonds between Turkish and British enterprises. Founded by Mohammed, whose multicultural heritage spans both Pakistani and British cultures, BMA represents the values we hold dear: integrity, loyalty, and mutual success.

Our name—BMA—honors the three pillars of our family: Begum, Mohammed, and Ayse. This family foundation gives us a unique perspective on the importance of trust and personal relationships in business, especially in Turkish culture where business is never just business—it's about people connecting with people.

With deep understanding of both Eastern and Western business approaches, we bridge cultural gaps that others simply cannot. When you work with BMA, you're not hiring consultants; you're gaining family allies committed to your success in the UK market.
